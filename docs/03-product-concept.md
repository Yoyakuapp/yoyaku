# Product Concept

## Overview

Yoyaku is a reservation platform designed around a single principle:

**Book in Seconds.**

Unlike conventional reservation systems that require users to browse multiple calendars, compare available times manually, or contact stores directly, Yoyaku enables users to discover real-time availability instantly and complete reservations with minimal interaction.

The platform prioritizes speed, simplicity, and reliability.

---

# Product Goals

The primary goals of Yoyaku are:

- Make reservations effortless.
- Reduce booking completion time.
- Eliminate unnecessary user input.
- Prevent reservation conflicts.
- Improve business efficiency.
- Increase customer satisfaction.
- Support scalable business operations.

---

# Core Concept

The reservation process is centered on user intent rather than store schedules.

Instead of asking users to navigate individual calendars, Yoyaku allows them to specify:

- Desired date
- Desired time
- Duration
- Number of people
- Preferred location
- Preferred service

The system then searches all available reservations and presents only bookable options.

---

# User Experience

The product experience is designed around four stages.

## Search

Users define what they want.

The system performs a real-time availability search.

---

## Compare

Available reservation slots are presented clearly.

Only valid reservation options are displayed.

---

## Reserve

Users confirm reservation details.

Required payments are processed if applicable.

---

## Complete

Reservations are confirmed immediately.

Confirmation notifications are delivered automatically.

---

# Product Characteristics

Yoyaku emphasizes the following characteristics.

## Speed

Reservations should be completed within one minute.

---

## Simplicity

Every screen should focus on a single purpose.

---

## Transparency

Only available reservation slots are displayed.

---

## Reliability

Double bookings must never occur.

---

## Scalability

The architecture supports expansion into multiple industries without redesign.

---

## Accessibility

The interface should remain understandable regardless of age or technical ability.

# Product Principles

Every feature introduced into Yoyaku should satisfy at least one of the following objectives:

- Reduce booking time.
- Improve usability.
- Improve operational efficiency.
- Increase booking completion.
- Improve customer satisfaction.
- Enhance scalability.
- Strengthen security.

Features that do not support these objectives should not be implemented.

# Product Vision

**Book in Seconds.**
