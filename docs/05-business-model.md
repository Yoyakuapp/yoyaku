# Business Model

## Overview

Yoyaku is a Software as a Service (SaaS) reservation platform that connects customers with reservation-based businesses through a fast, mobile-first booking experience.

The platform generates recurring revenue while helping businesses increase reservation efficiency, reduce administrative work, and improve customer satisfaction.

---

# Business Objectives

The primary business objectives are:

- Increase reservation completion rates.
- Improve customer retention.
- Increase business operational efficiency.
- Create recurring subscription revenue.
- Build a scalable global reservation platform.
- Support multiple industries using a single architecture.

---

# Customer Value Proposition

Customers receive:

- Fast reservation search
- Real-time availability
- Instant confirmation
- Simple mobile booking
- Secure payment
- Reservation history
- Easy cancellation

The entire reservation process should require the minimum number of user interactions.

---

# Business Value Proposition

Businesses receive:

- Online reservations
- Reservation automation
- Staff scheduling
- Customer management
- Sales reporting
- Business analytics
- Deposit management
- Cancellation management

The platform reduces manual administration while increasing operational efficiency.

# Revenue Model

The platform supports multiple revenue sources.

## Subscription

Examples:

- Starter
- Professional
- Business
- Enterprise

---

## Transaction Fee

Optional commission charged per completed reservation.

Applicable only when enabled.

## Payment Processing

# Success Metrics

Business success is measured using:

- Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)
- Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)
- Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
- Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)
- Reservation Completion Rate
- Conversion Rate
- Churn Rate
