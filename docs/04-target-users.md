# Target Users

## Overview

Yoyaku is designed for both customers seeking fast and convenient reservations and businesses requiring efficient reservation management.

The platform must provide an intuitive experience for every user regardless of technical knowledge.

---

# Primary User Groups

## Customers

Customers are individuals searching for available appointments.

Typical goals include:

- Finding available reservation times
- Comparing available options
- Making reservations quickly
- Managing existing reservations
- Receiving notifications
- Cancelling reservations when necessary

The customer experience should require minimal input while maintaining complete clarity.

---

## Store Owners

Store owners manage the overall operation of their businesses.

Responsibilities include:

- Store configuration
- Business hours
- Holiday settings
- Staff management
- Service management
- Reservation management
- Customer management
- Sales monitoring
- Business analytics

The administration interface should remain simple while providing complete operational control.

---

## Staff Members

Staff members provide reservation-based services.

Responsibilities include:

- Viewing schedules
- Managing personal availability
- Confirming reservations
- Updating reservation status
- Viewing customer information
- Managing working hours

Staff members should only have access to information required for their responsibilities.

---

## Platform Administrators

Platform administrators manage the Yoyaku platform itself.

Responsibilities include:

- User management
- Store approval
- Platform monitoring
- Security management
- Payment monitoring
- Incident management
- Content management
- System configuration

Administrative tools should prioritize operational efficiency and security.

---

# Customer Personas

## Busy Professionals

Characteristics:

- Limited free time
- Mobile-first usage
- Immediate booking expectations

Goals:

- Complete reservations within one minute.
- Receive immediate confirmation.
- Easily modify reservations.

---

## Families

Characteristics:

- Multiple participants
- Weekend reservations
- Schedule coordination

Goals:

- Search by number of people.
- Reserve suitable time slots.
- Manage family reservations.

---

## Repeat Customers

Characteristics:

- Frequent reservations
- Favorite stores
- Saved preferences

Goals:

- Rebook quickly.
- View reservation history.
- Receive loyalty benefits.

---

# Business Personas

## Small Business

Characteristics:

- One location
- Few staff members
- Simple operations

Needs:

- Easy setup
- Simple reservation management
- Low operational cost

---

## Medium Business

Characteristics:

- Multiple staff
- Higher reservation volume

Needs:

- Staff scheduling
- Reservation analytics
- Customer management

---

## Enterprise

Characteristics:

- Multiple locations
- Large workforce
- High transaction volume

Needs:

- Multi-store management
- Advanced reporting
- Role-based permissions
- High availability
- API integrations

---

# Accessibility Considerations

The platform should accommodate users with diverse needs.

Requirements include:

- Responsive design
- Keyboard accessibility
- Screen reader compatibility
- High contrast support
- Clear typography
- Touch-friendly interfaces

Accessibility should be incorporated throughout the design process.

---

# User Goals

Customers aim to:

- Search quickly
- Reserve easily
- Modify reservations
- Receive confirmations

Businesses aim to:

- Reduce administrative work
- Increase reservations
- Improve staff utilization
- Improve customer satisfaction
- Increase revenue

Platform administrators aim to:

- Maintain system stability
- Ensure security
- Monitor operations
- Resolve incidents efficiently

---

# Design Principles

Every user interface should:

- Minimize learning time.
- Reduce unnecessary interactions.
- Present only relevant information.
- Maintain consistency.
- Support mobile-first workflows.
- Provide immediate feedback.

All future development should prioritize the needs of these user groups.
