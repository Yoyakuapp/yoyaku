# Future Roadmap

## Overview

This document defines the long-term product roadmap for Yoyaku beyond Version 1.0.

The roadmap provides strategic direction for future development while preserving the platform's core philosophy:

**Book in Seconds.**

Future development shall prioritize scalability, automation, intelligence, and global expansion.

---

# Roadmap Principles

Every future feature should satisfy at least one of the following objectives.

- Improve booking speed.
- Improve customer experience.
- Improve business efficiency.
- Increase automation.
- Enhance scalability.
- Strengthen security.
- Expand platform capability.
